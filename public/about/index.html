At Godrej Design Lab we believe in the power of design to make meaningful change.

Godrej Design Lab, an initiative of Godrej & Boyce, is a platform to talk about design, how it impacts our life and how it can make it better. ​

Launched in 2014 by Navroze Godrej, today Godrej Design Lab continues with the intention of making life brighter and better through the power of well-designed objects, spaces, and systems. Our efforts are focused on engaging the community through though-provoking events, supporting upcoming talents and showcasing how design innovation can create better products and systems. ​

We are passionate about building meaningful relationships with creatives working across India.

​Godrej & Boyce has been part and parcel of life in India for more than 122 years, making homes safer, bringing together communities, creating gainful employment opportunities and shaping a sustainable world. ​

​We’re always looking at new and interesting ways to make an impact, if you have any ideas you’d like to share with us, please reach out on the contacts below.​
